Hello! I'm Jianghao

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Economics under the supervision of Professor Tae-Hwy Lee and Professor Aman Ullah at the University of California, Riverside. My research interests are nonparametric estimation and prediction with high dimensional data, machine learning methods for variable selection and classification, finite sample theory for inference under heteroskedasticity, and business analytics.

I am expected to get my Ph.D. in Economics and M.S. in Mathematics (Applied) in June 2019.
